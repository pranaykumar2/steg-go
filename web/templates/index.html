Privacy Policy

                    
Last updated: April 02, 2025

                

                
            


            

                

                    
Welcome to Steg-Go, a command-line and web-based tool that lets you hide encrypted messages inside ordinary images using advanced Least Significant Bit (LSB) Steganography combined with AES-256 encryption.

                    
Data Processing

                    
Steg-Go is designed with your privacy in mind:

                    
    
                        
  • Client-side Processing: All steganography and encryption operations are performed locally in your browser or on your device. Your images and messages are never transmitted to our servers.
    • 
                        
  • No Data Storage: We do not store your images, files, messages, or encryption keys. Everything stays on your device.
    • 
                        
  • No Tracking: We do not use cookies or tracking technologies to monitor your usage of the application.
    • 
                    

                    
Temporary Data

                    
For the web version of Steg-Go, your browser may temporarily store the following:

                    
    
                        
  • Images being processed for steganography
    • 
                        
  • Files being embedded
    • 
                        
  • Generated encryption keys
    • 
                    

                    
This information is only kept in the temporary memory of your browser and is deleted every 15 to 20 minutes while you are uploading.

                    
Encryption Keys

                    
Please note that Steg-Go generates a unique encryption key for each steganographic operation. We strongly recommend that you:

                    
    
                        
  • Save your encryption key in a secure location
    • 
                        
  • Do not share your encryption key with unauthorized individuals
    • 
                        
  • Understand that lost keys cannot be recovered, and data encrypted with a lost key cannot be decrypted
    • 
                    

                    
Security Measures

                    
Steg-Go uses military-grade AES-256 encryption to secure your data before embedding it in images. This ensures that even if steganography is detected, your hidden content remains securely encrypted.

                    
Third-Party Services

                    
Steg-Go does not integrate with or send data to any third-party services, analytics providers, or data processors.
